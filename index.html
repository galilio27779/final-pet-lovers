What are Pet Louvers?

Pet louvers are specially designed inserts that replace a section of your existing door or window blinds/louvers, providing a dedicated, secure passage for your pets. Unlike traditional pet doors that require cutting a large hole, pet louvers integrate discreetly and maintain the aesthetic appeal of your home. They are ideal for various types of pets, from small cats to medium-sized dogs.
